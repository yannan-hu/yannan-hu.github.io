Yannan Hu

PhD candidate in Public Health, Erasmus MC Rotterdam

Welcome to Yannan Hu's webpages.

I am a PhD candidate in Public Health at Erasmus Medical Center, Rotterdam, under the supervision of Johan P. Mackenbach and Frank J. van Lenthe. I will defend my PhD thesis on 14th, September 2016. My research interests are public health, social epidemiology, and applied econometrics.
